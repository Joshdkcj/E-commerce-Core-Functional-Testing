TC001
Positive test
Test goal: Search for products by keywords
Precondition: There are related products on the website
Test steps:
1. Open the website homepage.
2. Enter the product name.
3. Click the "Search" button.
Expected result: Display a list of products related to the keyword.



TC002
Abnormal test
Test goal: Search for non-existent products
Precondition: There are no related products on the website
Test steps:
1. Open the website homepage.
2. Enter the non-existent product name.
3. Click the "Search" button.
Expected result: Display the prompt "No related products found".
